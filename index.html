Welcome to Enoch Pratt, a new kind of library.

We want to help you find things to read, online and on paper. Research has never been easier than in the world of the internet, and we know just where to look. We revel in the smell of paper, the feel of a book, and a quiet afternoon spent reading, things we're happy to provide in our many locations. Our doors are open online and all over Baltimore.
